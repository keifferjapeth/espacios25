FAQ

Everything you need to know.

Short answers about data, privacy, and getting started with Espacios25.

How does the simulation overlay work?

We combine occupancy predictions with acoustic spill models to render a layered SVG overlay. Toggle modes or adjust sliders to see updates instantly.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All telemetry is encrypted in transit and at rest. Role-based access keeps simulation scopes isolated per workspace.

Can I import my floor plans?

Upload SVG or DXF files and map them to existing sensors. The overlay aligns automatically, letting you test automations on day one.

Do you support hardware on-prem?

Edge gateways buffer data during outages and sync securely when connectivity returns. Canvas tier includes on-prem deployment support.
