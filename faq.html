What is Espacios?

Espacios is the layer between demand and response. It captures leads across messaging and email, qualifies them with AI, routes ownership, and automates follow-ups.

Is it a chatbot?

No. Espacios orchestrates AI-first responses plus human ownership. Agents work from a shared inbox with full context and controls.

Do you replace CRM?

No. Your CRM stays the system of record. Espacios syncs contacts, ownership, and activity back to your CRM.

Can you do lead gen?

Yes. We capture inbound, enrich, score, and route to the right owner with automated follow-ups.

How does pricing work?

A base platform fee (AED 110 / month) plus add-ons. WhatsApp/Meta message fees are passed through.

Setup & onboarding?

Connect channels, define qualification, set routing rules, and go live. We provide playbooks for common flows.

Integrations?

CRM sync, webhooks, and automation tool triggers to fit your stack.

WhatsApp / Meta fees?

Pass-through. We do not mark them up.
